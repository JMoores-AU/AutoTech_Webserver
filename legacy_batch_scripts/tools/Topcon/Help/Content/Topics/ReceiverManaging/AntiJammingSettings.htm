Anti-Jamming Settings

Use Anti-jamming to detect the sources of jamming and to effectively mitigate them thus allowing the receiver to operate without degradation of positioning accuracy or loss-of-lock to the satellites. The following RF bands are available:

• GPS – monitors only GPS L1 and L2.
• GLONASS – monitors only GLONASS L1 and L2.
• L1 – monitors only GLONASS L1 and GPS L1.
• L2 – monitors only GLONASS L2 and GPS L2.
• Auto – automatically detects which bands are affected by interference and launches the jamming suppressor there.
• Off – anti-jamming is off.
• Manual – specifies the RF bands manually. You can select among GPS L1/L2/L5 and GLONASS L1/L2.
