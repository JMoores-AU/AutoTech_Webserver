Power

For HiPer HR:

The Power icon opens the Power dialog, which manages the charging of the HiPer HR receiver batteries and turns on/off the internal radio modem.

• Battery Charge Mode: — from the drop down list, you can select the following charging modes of internal and detachable batteries:
  • Off — the power supply unit does not charge the internal and detachable batteries of the receiver.
  • Auto — if the receiver is turned off, the power supply unit charges the internal and detachable batteries, if the receiver is turned on, the power supply unit charges the internal battery only.
  • A — the power supply unit always charges the internal battery only.
  • B — the power supply unit always charges the detachable battery only.
  • AB — the power supply unit always charges both batteries.
• Radio Modem check box — when selected, the software turns on the internal radio modem.
