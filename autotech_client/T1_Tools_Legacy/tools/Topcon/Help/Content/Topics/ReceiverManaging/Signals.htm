Signals

Specify which satellite signals the receiver will track. The following signals are available:

• GPS L1P – satellite signals in the GPS P1 channel.
• GPS L2P – satellite signals in the GPS P2 channel.
• GPS L2C-L – satellite signals in the GPS L2C-L channel.
• GPS L2C-M – satellite signals in the GPS L2C-M channel.
• GPS L5 – satellite signals in the GPS L5 channel.
• GLONASS L1P – satellite signals in the GLONASS P1 channel.
• GLONASS L2 P – satellite signals in the GLONASS P2 channel.
• GLONASS L2C/A – satellite signals in the GLONASS L2C/A channel.
• GLONASS L3 – satellite signals in the GLONASS L3 channel.
• Galileo E5a – satellite signals in the Galileo E5a channel.
• Galileo E5b – satellite signals in the Galileo E5b channel.
• Galileo E5 AltBoc – satellite signals in the Galileo AltBoc channel.
• Galileo E6 – satellite signals in the Galileo E6 channel.
• BeiDou B2 – satellite signals in the BeiDou B2 channel.
• BeiDou B3 – satellite signals in the BeiDou B3 channel.
• QZSS L1C – satellite signals in the QZSS L1C channel.
• QZSS L2C-L – satellite signals in the QZSS L2C-L channel.
• QZSS L2C-M – satellite signals in the QZSS L2C-M channel.
• QZSS L5 – satellite signals in the QZSS L5 channel.
• QZSS LEX Pilot – satellite signals in the QZSS LEX Pilot channel for 193 QZSS satellites’ PRN number.

The appearance of the satellite signals on the Signals tab and their tracking depend on the current OAF, hardware and firmware versions.
